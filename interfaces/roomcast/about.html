help

In this unit, you will be using the roomCast web system to do much of your work.

At the top of this window, you will see a status bar that indicates your class, the activity that you are working on, and the portal that you are using. Different kinds of users (students, teachers, and others) each have their own kinds of portals. Beneath the status bar, you will see a tool bar; when you click on an item in the tool bar, that tool will appear below (in this part of the screen). The tools you will be provided with will change depending on your activity and the portal that you are using.

When you see a question mark in the status bar, roomCast is waiting for you to make a selection before proceeding. You can "log in" to the system by selecting your portal; your teacher will tell you which one to use and provide you with a classword that you let you log into roomCast. Once you have selected your portal, you can select your 'name.' During later activities, your teacher may ask you to use a different portal.
